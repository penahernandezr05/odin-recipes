Waffles Recipe

About the recipe

This will be the best waffles you have ever tasted

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacture's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs in large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine.
  3. Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, salt, and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
  4. Spray preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto iron and cook until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
